CAREERS

Amadeus Music Center is an institution, established in 1992, dedicated to providing the best music education for children.

Amadeus Music is always looking for teachers who share our enthusiam in teaching to join our prestigious organization. AMC currently operates 3 locations in Queens: Flushing, Elmhurst, and Bayside and has over 500 students. It is one of the biggest and most reputable music schools in Queens and is highly regarded in the field of music education for children. AMC is conveniently located in Flushing, Queens, only 30 minutes away from Manhattan by subway.﻿
